### 2. Enhanced Generator Modal
Click the **Test Grid** button to open the new calibration tools:
- **Custom Limits**: Set your own Frequency (40-150kHz) and LPI ranges.
- **High LPI Mode**: Unlock LPI up to 4000 with a checkbox.
- **Grid Layout**: Customize cell sizes (down to 3mm) and gaps.
- **Engraving Settings**: Adjust base power and speed.
- **Live Preview**: See exactly how many test points you'll get.

### 3. Grid Analyzer (New!)
Upload a photo of your engraved grid to the **"Analyze Grid"** tab:
- **Auto-Detection**: The app reads the QR code to instantly recall the exact settings used.
- **Color Map**: Click interactively on the grid cells to see which Frequency/LPI settings produced that color.
- **Future Ready**: Ready for auto-color-matching in future updates.
